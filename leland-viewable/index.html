    Welcome

    The Sample Family Enterprise Group, founded in 1989, is a global advisory, education and research organization dedicated to helping enterprising families achieve meaningful and lasting success for their families, enterprises and financial wealth. Learn how SFEG assists families, ownership groups, and business leaders to achieve transformative change and multigenerational success.

    Advisory

    Sample Advisors to Family Enterprise is a highly specialized, international advisory firm serving family enterprises. It is devoted to helping families build shareholder value and achieve multigenerational success for their family, business, ownership group, family office, and financial wealth.

    Learn

    Education

    Sample Institute for Family Enterprise is an education and research institute dedicated to the significant issues family enterprises face around the world. It is a place where members of family enterprises come to learn, exchange ideas, develop themselves, and position their enterprises to be successful for generations.
